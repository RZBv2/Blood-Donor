- ✅ Modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
- ✅ No downloads, installations, or server setup needed!

### 3 Simple Steps to Get Started

**Step 1️⃣ — Open**
```
Simply double-click index.html or open it in your browser
```

**Step 2️⃣ — Search or Register**

## 📖 Detailed Usage Guide

### 🔎 Finding Blood Donors

1. Click the **"রক্ত প্রয়োজন"** (Find Blood) tab
2. Select your **blood type** from the dropdown
3. Choose your **area/location**
4. Click **Search** button
5. View matching donors and their contact information

**💡 Pro Tip:** Donors are instantly filtered by availability in your area!

### 📋 Registering as a Donor

1. Click the **"রক্তদাতা হন"** (Become Donor) tab
2. Fill in your details:
   - 👤 **Full Name**
   - 🩸 **Blood Type**
   - 📞 **Phone Number** (for contact)
   - 📍 **Area/Location**
3. Click **Register**
4. ✅ Success! You're now helping your community

### 🌐 Language & Theme Controls

Located in the **top-right corner:**

| Control | Options | What It Does |
|---------|---------|---|
| 🌍 **Language** | বাংলা / English | Switch interface language |
| 🌙 **Theme** | ☀️ Light / 🌙 Dark | Toggle display mode |

Your preferences are saved automatically!

## 💡 How Data Storage Works

### ✅ What Actually Happens

1. **Donor registers** → Data saved to browser's LocalStorage
2. **Search performed** → Results filtered from local data
3. **Browser closed** → Data persists (stays intact)
4. **Data never sent** → Remains completely on your device
